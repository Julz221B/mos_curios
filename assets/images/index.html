My name is Monique and I have lived in Albuquerque, NM for years. I grew up in Los Lunas, NM, just 30 minutes south of Albuquerque. I started Mos Curios in 2020 after I started creating jewelry and spell jars for my own use. I came up with the idea of sharing my talents and knowledge, and started the business on Facebook, through pop-up craft shows, and word of mouth. In April of 2021, I moved into my current building after building a friendship and partnership with Bobbie . We offer handmade metaphysical items for your everyday needs, as well as gift sets to share the magic with your friends and family! Also available will be tarot and rune readings, by appointment.
